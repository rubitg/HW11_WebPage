Summary: Lattitude vs. Maximum Temperature.

In order to analyze relationships between different climate measures and the geographical localization, this web page was created. The hypothesis to prove is the closeness of cities to latiude cero, generate higher temperatures. The same will be tested considering humidity, cloudiness and wind speed. This analysis was performed with a random sample of 500 cities round the world.The weather data collected and published by OpenWeatherMap API were used. Interesting results were obtained, so we invite you to visit this site.
