Summary: Latitude vs. Wind Speed



Latitude vs. Wind Speed was analyzed in order to find if there was a close relationship between both measures. From the random sample obtained of 500 cities in the world, a very weak negative correlationship was found: 18%. You can look at the graph and could observe this conclusion: Closer to the latitude 0 then a llittle bit weaker is wind speed.
