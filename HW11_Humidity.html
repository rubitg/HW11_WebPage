Summary: Latitude vs. Humidity



Latitude vs. Humidity was analyzed in order to find if there was a close relationship between both measures. From the random sample obtained of 500 cities in the world, a weak correlationship was found: less than 19%. You can look at the graph and could observe this conclusion: Plots are mostly everywere so same humidity levels can be present no matter the latitude.
